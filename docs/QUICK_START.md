# Quick Start Guide - Launch Your Token in 5 Minutes

## What You'll Need

- **Solana Wallet** (Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack)
- **0.1 SOL** (~$10 USD) for launch + first buy
- **Token details** (name, symbol, logo)

---

## Step 1: Connect Wallet (30 seconds)

1. Visit [launchpad.example.com](https://launchpad.example.com)
2. Click "Connect Wallet" in top-right
3. Select your wallet (Phantom recommended)
4. Approve connection

‚úÖ **You're connected!**

---

## Step 2: Create Your Token (2 minutes)

1. Click **"Launch Token"** button
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Name:** Your token's full name (e.g., "Moon Shot")
   - **Symbol:** 3-5 letters (e.g., "MOON")
   - **Description:** What your token is about
   - **Logo:** Upload square image (PNG/JPG, max 2MB)

3. Click **"Create Token"**

4. **Sign transaction** in wallet:
   - Cost: 0.05 SOL
   - Confirms in ~1 second

‚úÖ **Your token is live!**

---

## Step 3: Make First Purchase (1 minute)

To kickstart trading, buy some of your own token:

1. Enter amount (e.g., 0.1 SOL = ~$10)
2. Click **"Buy"**
3. Sign transaction
4. **Done!** You now own your tokens

### Bonding Curve Phase
- Token starts trading at low price

- Automatically moves to Meteora DLMM
- Full professional trading platform
- Liquidity locked (anti-rug)
- You continue earning fees

## Common Questions

**Q: How much does it cost?**  
A: 0.05 SOL (~$5) one-time launch fee.

**Q: When do I start earning?**  
A: From the first trade! 50% of fees go to you.

**Q: Can I set my own price?**  
A: No, the bonding curve determines price based on demand.

**Q: What if my token doesn't reach 10 SOL?**  
A: It stays on the curve and continues trading. No deadline.

**Q: Can I remove liquidity?**  
A: No. 10% is locked forever, 45% is your LP share (not removable). This protects holders.

**Q: Is this safe?**  
A: Built-in anti-rug protections, but all crypto is high risk. DYOR.
